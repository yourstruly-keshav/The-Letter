Hey — Titli

I know you don't want to talk with me, and I kinda deserve that.

I didn't mean to hurt you — but my actions are never in sync with my intentions. In retrospect, I shouldn't have bombarded you with those questions. I was making you feel even more overwhelmed than you already were.

I know I messed it up. And I don't want any answers — I'm not reaching out to talk about what happened. Let the past be in the past. Forget about it.

I just want to talk to my best friend. The person who knew me the best. There is no one to blame for what happened — it was bound to happen. But it will be both of our fault if this becomes permanent and we stop talking altogether.

I made a huge mistake — falling in love with the person I could talk to about anything, and ruining it all. I've accepted what happened. I've made peace with the idea that we might never end up together. I just can't let a good friend walk. Please — there is too much happening, and I need to talk with you.

always,
Keshav
I am truly sorry for everything- I won't make the same mistakes.
